# KineticZoneYard - Product Requirements Document
## Vision & Philosophy
KineticZoneYard is designed to democratize elite physical execution. It is built on the absolute belief that true wellness—encompassing body, mind, and soul—requires liberation from commercial gym models and expensive, proprietary machinery. KZY uses calisthenics and intelligent spatial analysis to deliver health sovereignty directly to the user.
## The 5 Onboarding Journey Pillars
1. Core Biometrics & Goals: Intentional physical alignment focusing on muscle growth, structural stability, or raw strength priority.
2. Spatial Assets: Identifying the immediate training theater: Pure Indoor, Pure Outdoor, or a Hybrid Split (Garage/Patio layouts).
3. Hardware Stack: Maximizing minimal tools: pure bodyweight, calisthenics anchors (rings/bars), variable resistance bands, or selective heavy loads.
4. Milestone Targets: Progressive milestones tracking master bodyweight maneuvers (Pull-ups, Deep Push-ups, Handstands, Pistol Squats).
5. Time Window Optimization: Intentionally structuring exposure around the mid-day UVB window (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM) to promote vital vitamin D synthesis alongside physical output.
